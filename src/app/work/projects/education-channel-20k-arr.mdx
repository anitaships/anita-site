## Context

We needed revenue channels beyond self-serve signups. Education partners — training organizations teaching cross-border e-commerce — were a promising but unproven fit.

## What I did

Owned one education channel end-to-end: from the first cold outreach and BD, to onboarding, to launch and conversion.

## How

- Identified and reached out to the partner; negotiated the collaboration terms.
- Designed the funnel from referral to paid signup.
- Supported users through onboarding to keep drop-off low.

## Outcome

- **275 signups / 240 paid — an 87% conversion rate.**
- **~$20K ARR** from a single channel I ran solo.

## Why it matters

I can take a channel from zero — cold outreach to closed revenue — without a playbook handed to me.
