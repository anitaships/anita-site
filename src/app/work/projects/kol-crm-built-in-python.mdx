## Context

Managing creator (KOL) relationships across 5 platforms meant scattered DMs, half-broken spreadsheets, and a $140/mo SaaS stack that still didn't fit how we actually worked.

## What I did

Built a lightweight CRM as a Python CLI to track every creator, every conversation, and every pipeline stage in one place.

## How

- Modeled the creator pipeline: sourcing → outreach → negotiation → live → archived.
- Stored everything in version-controlled data files — no vendor lock-in, full ownership of the data.
- Wired in email outreach scheduling so follow-ups didn't slip.

## Outcome

- **89 creators across 5 platforms** fully archived and trackable.
- Replaced a **$140/mo SaaS stack** with a free, tailored tool.
- Email outreach ran at a **30–50% open rate** (vs. 20–25% industry average).

## Why it matters

When the off-the-shelf tool doesn't fit, I build the one that does — and keep the data in my own hands.
