## Context

A cross-border AI product needs real users to talk to. We started with nothing — no place for overseas TikTok sellers to gather, give feedback, or stick around.

## What I did

Built and ran an overseas seller community on Discord from scratch — and standardized it so it could run without me babysitting it every day.

## How — 4 automated SOPs

- **Onboarding flow** to welcome and orient new members.
- **Daily intel push** with market and product updates.
- **Member-stats bot** tracking growth and activity.
- **Structured feedback forms** to capture seller input.

## Outcome

- **0 → 335 members in 11 months.**
- **4 automated SOPs** keeping the community running on its own.
- 1:1 onboarded 10+ seed users and interviewed sellers across **10+ countries in English** — feeding insights straight back to product.

## Why it matters

Community isn't just headcount — it's a feedback loop. I turned ours into a steady source of product insight and retention.
